* Project: Unified User IO (UU-IO)
** Global Principle: Least-Able First
*** Statement
- The operative ceiling is set by the *least-able user’s* degree and control.
- All modalities must degrade gracefully to binary scanning and dwell-activation.
- Preference order (default): =Radial text-tokens= → Scanning → Keystroke/Completion → Others.
*** WAI-ARIA Scaffold
- Roles: =button=, =menu=, =menuitemradio=, =combobox=, =option=, =dialog=, =status=, =progressbar=.
- Live regions: =aria-live= polite for prompts; assertive only for safety/critical.
- Focus model: =aria-activedescendant= for gaze/scan; roving tabindex for radial menus.
*** Non-Functional Constraints
- Latency budgets per modality; fallbacks within 2 steps.
- Privacy & safety for bio/neural streams; consent + local processing preference.
- Internationalization: token lexicons, phoneme sets, braille tables, sign-language hooks.

** Primary UI: Radial Pop-Out Context Menu
*** Interaction Model
- Token wheel with sectors (primary intents), rings (depth), and slices (tokens).
- Activation: switch scan, dwell, gaze-confirm, click/tap, voice keyword.
*** Radial Text-Tokens Input (Preferred)
- Token grammar: [[IO Patterns → Symbolic IO]]; device-agnostic; predictive =Completion= optional.
- Visual affordances: high-contrast, large targets, motor-tolerant spacing.
*** Audio Coexistence
- Procedural mixer must duck/sidechain around speech input/output; no masking.
- Earcons map to state changes (enter/confirm/cancel/error) with tactile mirrors.

** Deliverables
*** Specs
- =uu-io.radial-spec.org=, =uu-io.aria-map.json=, =uu-io.hifi.flows.puml=
*** Prototypes
- Minimal scanning radial (binary switch), touch/gaze variant, voice-augmented variant.
*** Verification
- Scenario matrix across abilities; WCAG 2.2/ARIA-AT runs; latency + error recovery tests.

* Input Archetype Patterns
** Symbolic (Keystroke / Completion / Token)
*** Devices/Techniques
- Physical/adaptive/chorded keyboards; on-screen keyboards; token-wheel selection; IDE/code completion; language models for next-token hints.
*** Accessibility Modes
- Scanning keyboards; dwell type; sticky modifiers; large-key overlays.
** Spatial Pointing
*** Devices/Techniques
- Mouse/trackball/vertical mice; touchpads; trackpoints; 3D mice.
*** Accessibility Modes
- Acceleration curves, snap-to-target, enlarged hit regions.
** Direct Touch
*** Devices/Techniques
- Capacitive touchscreens; pen displays; interactive whiteboards.
*** Accessibility Modes
- Lift-to-confirm, palm rejection, edge guards, one-handed reach zoning.
** Gesture & Motion
*** Devices/Techniques
- Camera/body tracking; IMU armbands; VR finger tracking.
*** Accessibility Modes
- Low-amplitude gestures; seated-only sets; pose hold-to-confirm.
** Haptic/Glove
*** Devices/Techniques
- Data gloves; force/micro-haptic gloves; exoskeletal assist.
*** Accessibility Modes
- Adaptive force thresholds; tremor filtering.
** Voice & Breath
*** Devices/Techniques
- ASR command/dictation; wake words; sip-and-puff (binary/analog).
*** Accessibility Modes
- Noise-robust grammars; cough/effort safeguards; silent-click phonemes.
** Gaze & Orientation
*** Devices/Techniques
- Eye trackers; head trackers; dwell and saccade gestures.
*** Accessibility Modes
- Anti-Midas touch, confirmation halos, blink-filtering.
** Neural/Biosignal
*** Devices/Techniques
- EEG/EMG; tongue/intraoral; bio-switches.
*** Accessibility Modes
- Rapid calibration; confidence-gated actions; safe fallback to scanning.
** Tangible/Physical Objects
*** Devices/Techniques
- Token-on-surface; knobs/dials; tagged props.
*** Accessibility Modes
- Large-form tokens; magnetic alignment; audible confirmations.
** Binary/Discrete Switching
*** Devices/Techniques
- Single/multi-switch; pedals; shoulder bumpers.
*** Accessibility Modes
- Auto-scan; step-scan; error-forgiving backtrack.

* IO Patterns
** Symbolic IO
*** Inputs
- Token-wheel selection; Keystroke; Completion; Voice dictation.
*** Outputs
- Text/braille; polite =aria-live= prompts; earcons/tactile confirms.
*** UI Contract
- Radial Pop-Out Menu as primary; scanning + dwell; undo/confirm pattern.
** Spatial IO
*** Inputs
- Point-and-plane; direct touch.
*** Outputs
- Focus rings, cursor magnets, target previews.
*** UI Contract
- Radial menu at cursor/focus; Fitts-optimized target sizing.
** Gesture ↔ Haptic IO
*** Inputs
- Camera/IMU gestures; glove articulation.
*** Outputs
- Haptic confirms; force fences; low-vision complements.
*** UI Contract
- Gesture grammar with discoverable prompts; error-tolerant holds.
** Voice ↔ Audio IO
*** Inputs
- ASR commands; breath control.
*** Outputs
- TTS, spatial prompts, sidechain ducking; transcript pane.
*** UI Contract
- Barge-in allowed; confirmation echoes; no mixer masking.
** Gaze ↔ Visual Emphasis IO
*** Inputs
- Gaze focus; head pose.
*** Outputs
- Highlight rails; auto-scroll; dwell timers.
*** UI Contract
- Anti-Midas dwell-confirm; glance-to-preview, act-to-commit.
** Neural/Biofeedback IO
*** Inputs
- EEG/EMG/tongue; bio-switch.
*** Outputs
- Confidence meters; gentle haptics; private local visualizations.
*** UI Contract
- Fast calibration; privacy by default; safe fallback to Binary/Scanning.
** Binary/Scanning IO
*** Inputs
- Single/multi-switch; pedal; shoulder.
*** Outputs
- Strong tactile/audio confirms; clear focus indication.
*** UI Contract
- Auto/step scan; reversible steps; timeouts with resume.
